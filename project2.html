Poster Design

Illustrator

This piece was created in exploration of Adobe Illustrator. Based on the short story "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty" the artist wanted to include illustrations of key plot points as the short story is a timeless classic. This piece is formed by photographs that were edited to look similar in style and color palete and while perhaps to bland for a movie poster I think it makes a great book cover.
